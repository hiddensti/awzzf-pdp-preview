Three questions

Things people ask first.

What is flow-forming?
A cast wheel blank gets spun on a mandrel while rollers compress the rim under heat. Aluminum grain aligns and densifies — same load rating as the cast it started from, with less material. Lighter wheel, stronger rim.
How does this compare to AWZZ Forged?
AWZZ is forged from a solid billet — lightest and stiffest wheel we make, top-tier price. AWZZF gets you closer to that on a daily-driven Tesla 3/Y for meaningfully less. Both ship with our Lifetime Structural Warranty and 50%-off accidental damage program.
What does "ships end of July" mean exactly?
Wheels arrive at our Denton, TX warehouse from the factory by end of July. Once they land, we charge the remaining balance (if you used $10/wheel partial payment) and ship to you within 2 business days, in the order pre-orders were placed.
